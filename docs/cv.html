Education

Doctor of Philosophy — Nutrition & Exercise Physiology

The University of Western Australia | 2020–2024

PhD Title: “Iron and women’s health: A multidimensional exploration of iron deficiency, management, and treatment in active females”

This thesis provides a multidimensional understanding of iron-deficiency in women. Initial research systematically reviews contemporary literature, challenging diagnostic standards and evaluating treatment efficacy. Key insights were gained through targeted screening, revealing high prevalence and interplay between exercise, menstrual health, and iron metabolism among women. By integrating perspectives and preferences of women with empirical evidence, advancements were made in tailoring treatment approaches, underscoring the efficacy of intravenous-iron-therapy. Findings suggest a paradigm shift in iron-deficiency management for women.
